About

Get to know me a little better!

Education

I graduated from the University of Nevada, Reno with a Bachelor's Degree in Education in 1989 and a Master's Degree in Creative Arts in Education in 1994.

Work Experience

I have worked the past 28 years as an elementary school teacher at the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd grade level, and have taught various subjects including social studies, mathematics, science, reading, and writing.
I have been teaching children English with VIP Kid for the past 6 months. My sessions are 30 minutes long where I implement various props and slideshows to help the children I tutor better connect the material to real life objects, places and things.

Personal Life

I have two children and three dogs. A 23 year-old son and a 19 year-old daughter, as well as a Pug, English Golden Retriever and West Highland Terrier. In my free time, I enjoy spending time with my family and going on hikes with all three of my dogs.
